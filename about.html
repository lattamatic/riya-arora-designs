About Riya

Designer, storyteller, and keeper of Indian textile traditions.

🧵

The Woman Behind the Thread

Riya Arora grew up watching her grandmother drape sarees with effortless grace — each fold a ritual, each motif a memory. That childhood wonder became a lifelong calling.

After graduating from the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Mumbai, Riya spent three years apprenticing under master weavers in Varanasi and Kanchipuram, learning the language of silk and zari firsthand.

"I don't just design clothes. I design the feeling a woman carries when she walks into a room."

Today, Riya works directly with every client — listening, sketching, refining — before passing the design to her trusted team of artisans. Her role is the bridge between your dream and the hands that bring it to life.
