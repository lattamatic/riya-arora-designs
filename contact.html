Common Questions

How long does a custom order take?

Typically 6–10 weeks depending on the complexity. Bridal sets may take up to 12 weeks. We recommend reaching out 3–4 months before your event.

Do you ship outside Mumbai?

Yes — we ship pan-India and internationally. Consultations can be done via video call for outstation clients.

What is the minimum budget for a custom piece?

Our custom designs start at ₹25,000 for sarees and ₹40,000 for lehengas, depending on fabric and embroidery choices.

Can I bring my own fabric?

Absolutely. Many clients bring heirloom fabric — a grandmother's saree, for instance — and we incorporate it into a new design.
